In the world of entertainment, one name that has left an indelible mark is that of Harry Melling. Born as Harry Edward Melling on March 13, 1989, in London, Britain, this 33-year-old actor has garnered acclaim for his versatile roles in both film and television. Best known for portraying Dudley Dursley in the iconic Harry Potter film series, Melling has proven his acting prowess in various genres over the years.

Who is Harry Melling?

Harry Melling, hailing from a family with a strong connection to the arts, is the son of Joanna Troughton and Brian Melling. Raised alongside his brother Jack Melling, Harry had a supportive upbringing that fueled his passion for acting. His mother, a children’s book author, and his father, a visual designer, contributed to Melling’s artistic inclinations. Additionally, as the grandson of Patrick Troughton and Shelagh Robbery, he inherits a rich artistic heritage.

Tragic Loss: Pastors Wife Killed by Stray Bullet While Asleep


The untimely death of a woman who was hit by a stray bullet while sleeping in her own home has left her family devastated.

Lashunda Ellison tragically succumbed to injuries sustained when she was shot in the head last week. Her husband, Mac, discovered her unresponsive after the shooting, as reported by WSB-TV.

Mac, awakening in the dead of night, found his wife in critical condition and immediately called for assistance from their children to move her.

Narrating the harrowing experience to WSB-TV, Mac shared, “I just woke up to very heavy breathing. And we saw blood everywhere.”

Mac, a pastor at Temple of Faith Ministries, informed his followers on Facebook that his wife passed away on Monday, October 10, saying, “My wife Lashunda Heath-Ellison gained her wings. I believe to be absent from the body is to be present with the Lord. Please give us a moment to process this. My Perfect Angel is gone but forever in our hearts.”

Upon her arrival at the hospital, doctors discovered a bullet lodged in Lashunda’s head. The family later found a hole in the wall caused by the same bullet, as reported by WSB-TV.

“It went through the headboard and right into the top of my wife’s head,” Mac recounted to the station.

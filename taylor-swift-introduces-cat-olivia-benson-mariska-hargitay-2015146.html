Taylor Swift Introduces Cat Olivia Benson to Mariska Hargitay

Perhaps she can teach her some Law & Order? Taylor Swift introduced her thousands of fans to Mariska Hargitay at one of her Philadelphia shows on her 1989 World Tour. But there was one introduction in particular that sent fans into a frenzy. 

Longtime Law & Order fan Swift introduced Hargitay, who plays Sergeant Olivia Benson, to her cat Olivia Benson. 

Thankfully Tay captured the moment in an Instagram video, posting it on June 13.

Ana de Armas buys $7m home in rural Vermont after privacy issues

The Knives Out actress reportedly moved to her new home in November

    Ana de Armas values her privacy so much, she's reportedly bought a $7 million luxury home in rural Vermont to escape prying eyes.

    The actress – who fled LA following her failed romance with Ben Affleck after she was hounded by paparazzi – is said to have splashed out on a fully-furnished, six-bedroom, eight-bathroom home that sits on 30 acres of land.

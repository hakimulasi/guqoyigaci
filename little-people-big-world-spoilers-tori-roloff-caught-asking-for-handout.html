Tori Roloff Caught Asking For Handout

Little People, Big World (LBPW) spoilers reveal that Tori Roloff was caught asking for handouts. She ticked off fans with her social media request. Most of them took to Reddit to share their thoughts. Read on to learn what happened.

LBPW Spoilers: Tori And Zach Roloff’s 2024 Net Worth

The Little People, Big World spoilers suggest that fans want to know Tori and Zach Roloff’s 2024 net worth, according to Life & Style Magazine. The couple just launched their new podcast Raising Heights, which landed at the top of the charts. The TLC stars are also successful influencers.

According to a new report, they have an estimated net worth of $700k. This isn’t as close to Amy Roloff or Matt Roloff’s 2024 net worth, but it’s more than what the typical family makes these days, especially in this fragile economy.

The couple doesn’t look like they need financial help. Tori’s bold move has been rubbing fans the wrong way.

Little People, Big World Spoilers: Tori Roloff Asks For Freebies

Tori Roloff was looking for freebies. She took to her Instagram Stories to ask her millions of followers to help her out. Tori wrote: “If anybody knows how to get 3 tickets to sweethearts night tomorrow at Disneyland, please DM me!” The reality star kept her DMs open but probably got some hate.

LBPW fans flocked to Reddit when they learned about her social media post. Tori wanted to get information from her followers so she could get her hands on tickets. However, some fans took it as if she was looking for a handout.

LBPW Fans Call Out TLC Star’s Entitlement

It’s unclear whether Tori was looking for free tickets to Disneyland. This brought up a heavy debate on a Reddit thread. One fan wrote: “Come on, Tori. I was just starting to like you.”

Another user noted: “ I was liking the podcast, too. Why anyone feels like they’re so entitled to mobilize their followers to get them stuff is beyond me. Maybe exercise a little forethought and planning and buy your tickets like everyone else. The level of entitlement is so weird.”

Sweetheart Nite is a part of the Disneyland After Dark set of events and runs through Valentine’s Day. These after-hour events take place at Disneyland theme parks and include exclusive entertainment, characters, and more. The official website calls it a “celebration of love.” Tickets range from $139 to $169 per guest and don’t include parking.

Accreditation:

NCTI is nationally accredited by the Commission on the Accreditation of Allied Health Education Programs (CAAHEP), and has grown into the largest paramedic training institution in the United States.

National College of Technical Instruction, Roseville:

The National College of Technical Instruction (NCTI) is dedicated to the delivery of high-quality education in the field of Emergency Medical Services (EMS) in several locations in California as well as throughout the United States. The national headquarters for NCTI are located in Roseville in the metropolitan area of Sacramento, CA. Since 1988, NCTI has been offering specialized instructional programs, including Emergency Medical Technician, Paramedic, and other emergency services and critical care programs.

Emergency Medical Services (EMS) Courses:

NCTI programs are designed to prepare students to function as qualified entry-level healthcare professionals, providing the cognitive, psychomotor and affective skills necessary to successfully qualify for standardized certification and licensing examinations for EMS personnel throughout the United States. Our staff and faculty partner with students to achieve academic success.

Courses include:
– EMT (Basic, Intermediate, Paramedic)
– Prehospital Trauma Life Support
– Basic Cardiac Life Support (CPR) Provider
– Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Provider
– Advanced Medical Life Support Provider
– Pediatric Advanced Life Support Provider
– 12-lead EKG
– Critical Care Transport-Paramedic
– National Registry Paramedic Preparation
– National Registry Skills Testing

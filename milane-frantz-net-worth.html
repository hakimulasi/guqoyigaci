What is Milane Frantz's Net Worth?

Milane Frantz is an American heiress who has a net worth of $7 billion. Together with her siblings, Dannine Avara, Randa Williams and Scott Duncan, Milane Frantz inherited an enormous fortune from their late father, founder of Enterprise Products. Last year, each of their fortune increased when the company's shares elevated by almost 24%. Today, the company is an energy pipeline empire with over 50,000 miles of natural oil, gas and petrochemical pipelines, what makes it one of the most successful pipeline companies. Moreover, Frantz is a member of the board of Hermann Park Conservancy in Houston and the Baylor College of Medicine Board of Trustees.

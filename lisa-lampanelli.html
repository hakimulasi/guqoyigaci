Lisa Lampanelli

Lampanelli in 2012, image via Debby Wong/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Lisa Marie Lampugnale

Place of Birth: Trumbull, Connecticut, U.S.

Date of Birth: July 19, 1961

Ethnicity: Italian, Polish

Lisa Lampanelli is an American stand-up comedian, actress, and insult comic. She is known as the Queen of Mean.

She has said that she is of three quarters Italian descent.

Lisa’s paternal grandfather was named Angelo Lampugnale.

Lisa’s paternal grandmother was named Grace Burke.

Lisa’s maternal grandfather was Stanley B. Velgot (the son of Frank Velgot and Antonia Cienetz). Stanley was born in Pennsylvania, to Polish parents.

Lisa’s maternal grandmother was Marie Rickey (the daughter of Frank Rickey and Nancy Cuozzo). Marie was born in Pennsylvania. Frank was Italian, the son of Pasqua Rickey and Mary. Nancy was of Italian descent.

Ciara's Son Future Dances Adorably to Her New Song: Watch

Mama’s biggest fan! Ciara took to Instagram on Sunday, April 19, to share an adorable video of her son Future Jr. grooving along to her music. 

The cute tot, whom Ciara welcomed with then-fiancé rapper Future in May 2014, was celebrating his 11-month birthday. Now sporting two tiny bottom teeth, the little one also has a full head of curly hair and a sweet, contagious smile.

“Dancin Dancin Dancin!:) Happy 11 Months Juicy Man,” Ciara captioned the video of Future Jr. dancing. “Song- #AllGood #Jackie.”

The video shows young Future stomping his feet and moving to the music in a white cotton onesie. With his hands on a suitcase, the little boy smiles as he kicks his feet faster and faster.

Ciara, 29, used the clip not only to celebrate her son, but also to tease a new song off of her upcoming album Jackie. Due out in just two weeks, the singer’s sixth studio album includes the featured track “All Good.”

Led by the single “I Bet,” Jackie is the “Body Party” singer’s first project since she and ex Future, 31, parted ways. The two previously worked on music together, recording the song “Where You Go” for Ciara’s self-titled 2013 album. 

“It clearly didn’t work out,” Ciara told The Breakfast Club last week of her engagement. “But I have the greatest gift, the greatest joy of my life is my son. Honestly, I feel like everything in life happens for a reason and my son has been the greatest gift that God has given me in my life and been the most game-changing thing that’s happened to my life, in a necessary way.”

How did Joe Maddon start his Professional Career?

In 1975, as a free agent, Maddon commenced his professional baseball career in Minor League Baseball, where he played catcher for the California Angels.

During his 31-year tenure with the Angels, Maddon held various positions of authority, including minor league manager, scout, itinerant minor league hitting instructor, and major league team coach.

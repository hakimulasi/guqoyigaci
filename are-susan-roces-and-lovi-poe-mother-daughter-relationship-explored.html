Are Susan Roces and Lovi Poe Mother & Daughter?

Rumors indicate that Susan Roces and Lovi Poe are mother and daughter. However, they are not biologically related. Lovi is the daughter of Susan’s late husband, Fernando Poe Jr.

Fernando was married to actress Roces since 1968, but he had affairs out of his marriage with two other women. One of them is Anna Marin, and another is Rowena Moran.

Lovi is the daughter of Rowena and Fernando. On the other hand, Susan’s child is Grace, whom she and her partner adopted. Thus, the actress did not give birth to any child during her lifetime.

Additionally, there is not much information on her relationship with her stepchildren when she was alive. But, there were few pictures where they were seen celebrating Christmas together. Moreover, the children lost their father in 2004 because of cerebral thrombosis with multiple organ failure.

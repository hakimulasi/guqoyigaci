List of Universities in Imo State

Imo State federal, state and private universities.

Four universities exist in Imo State, of which one belongs to the federal government, two belong to the state government while one is privately-owned.

Below is the list of NUC approved Federal, state and private universities in Imo State.

Universities in Imo State

Federal:

  • Federal University of Technology, Owerri

State:

  • Imo State University, Owerri
  • Eastern Palm University Ogboko, Imo State

Private:

  • Hezekiah University, Umudi

What is Ana Maria Brescia Cafferata's Net Worth?

Ana Maria Brescia Cafferata is a wealthy Peruvian heir who has a net worth of $1.7 billion. Born to Fortunato Brescia Tassano, the man who built a real estate company that capitalized on Lima's growth, Ana Maria was one of the rightful heirs to inherit his wealth after his death in 1951. Today, her assets are managed by her brothers, Pedro and Mario, who also took just portions of their affluent father's possessions.

They pull the strings of companies know as Grupo Brescia, with interests in various industries including agriculture, fish products, cement, paint products, explosives, welding, insurance, banking, mining and real estate. With 30% of Grupo Brescia, Ana Maria Brescia Cafferata has become one of Peru's richest people.

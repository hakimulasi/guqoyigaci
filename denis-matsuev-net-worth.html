FAQs about Denis Matsuev

Who is Denis Matsuev?

He is a renowned Russian pianist known for his powerful performances and technical prowess. He gained international fame after winning the 11th International Tchaikovsky Competition in 1998.

What are some of Matsuev’s notable performances?

Matsuev has performed with many of the world’s leading orchestras, including the New York Philharmonic and the Mariinsky Orchestra, under the baton of famous conductors like Valery Gergiev and Zubin Mehta.

Has Matsuev won any awards?

Yes, among other accolades, Matsuev has received the Shostakovich Prize, the State Prize of the Russian Federation in Literature and Arts, and he was named a People’s Artist of Russia in 2011.

What music festivals does Matsuev participate in?

Matsuev is a regular at many prestigious music festivals around the world, including the Verbier Festival in Switzerland, the Hollywood Bowl in the USA, and the Stars of the White Nights Festival in Russia.

What educational and charitable work is Matsuev involved in?

Matsuev leads several music festivals and educational projects, including the Stars on Baikal festival in his hometown of Irkutsk and the Crescendo music festival held in various international cities.

Does Matsuev have any recordings?

Matsuev has numerous recordings to his name, including works by Rachmaninoff, Tchaikovsky, and Shostakovich, released under labels like Sony BMG and Mariinsky.

Is Denis Matsuev married?

Yes, he is married to Yekaterina Shipulina.

Where is Denis Matsuev from?

What are Denis Matsuev’s contributions to music competitions?

Matsuev has been the artistic director for several international piano competitions, including the Astana Piano Passion and the Grand Piano Competition in Moscow.

What is Matsuev’s educational background?

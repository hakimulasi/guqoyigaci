Why does Gilbert Gottfried talk like that?

Gilbert Jeremy Gottfried, an American stand-up comedian, and actor who played the voice of the parrot Iago in the Disney film “Aladdin,” has passed on at 67 on Sunday.

Gottfried was born on February 28, 1955, in Brooklyn, New York. He started doing comedy at age 15, in New York City and, after a few years, he was nicknamed “the comedian’s comedian”.

Gilbert Gottfried was married to Dara Kravitz who he met at a Grammy Awards party and they later got married in 2007.

The couple has two children, a daughter named Lily Aster Goffried, and a son, Max Aaron Gottfried.

Why does Gilbert Gottfried talk like that?

Gilbert Jeremy Gottfried’s style of comedy featured an exaggerated shrill voice and emphasis on crude humor.

Gottfried was a unique comedian known for speaking in a loud and grating voice, which was not his natural speaking voice.

He played the voice of the different characters in films including Aladin, and Beverly hills Corp II among others.

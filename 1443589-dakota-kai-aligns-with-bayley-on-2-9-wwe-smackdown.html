Dakota Kai Aligns With Bayley On 2/9 WWE SmackDown

Dakota Kai chooses her side.

During the February 9 episode of WWE SmackDown, Royal Rumble winner Bayley appeared in the ring to address the Damage CTRL breakup when Dakota Kai interrupted and addressed her confusion over what happened on February 2.

Kai suggested that Damage CTRL’s demise was because of Kairi Sane and Asuka’s recent involvement and that she misses the original trio. When Bayley asked Kai where she stands, IYO SKY, Sane, and Asuka circled the ring. Kai grabbed a chair and seemed poised to strike Bayley when she opted to fend off the other three from the ring.

To solidify where she stands, Kai posted, “BayKota 4 ever” on social media right after the segment.

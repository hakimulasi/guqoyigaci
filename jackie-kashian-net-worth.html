Her journey took a pivotal turn at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where the allure of stand-up comedy captivated her.

FAQs about Jackie Kashian

Who is Jackie Kashian?

Jackie Kashian is an American stand-up comedian known for her engaging humor and extensive touring schedule. She has been seen on various platforms including Conan, Corden, HBO, Netflix, Comedy Central, and has also been heard on NPR and Audible.

What are some career highlights of Jackie Kashian?

Kashian has had a half-hour special on Comedy Central and has made appearances on CBS, NBC, and the nationally syndicated Radio/TV show Bob And Tom. She tours internationally, spending about 25 weeks a year on the road, and has entertained US troops stationed in Africa and the Middle East multiple times.

Is she of Armenian descent?

Yes, she is of Armenian and Irish descent. She was born in South Milwaukee, Wisconsin, and raised by her father, Elliot, and her stepmother, Nancy, alongside her four older brothers and one older sister.

Who is she married to?

Has she acted in television or films?

Yes, she has acted in the TV series Murphy Brown (1988) and in films such as Bam Bam and Celeste (2005) and Looking Forward: 2016 (2018).

She hosts The Dork Forest where she interviews guests about their interests and passions. She is also a host on The Jackie and Laurie Show and has made guest appearances on podcasts like Doug Loves Movies, and Jordan, Jesse, GO!.

How long has she been performing stand-up comedy?

She has been performing stand-up comedy for over 35 years, during which her albums have achieved significant success, being #1 on Amazon and iTunes and amassing over 10 million listens.

What is her connection to the military?

She has performed several times for US troops stationed in Africa and the Middle East, showcasing her support and appreciation for the military through her comedy.

What is the significance of her album It is Never Going to be Bread?

Her album It is Never Going to be Bread was named one of the top comedy albums of 2010 by Amazon.com, highlighting her talent and the positive reception of her work in the comedy world.

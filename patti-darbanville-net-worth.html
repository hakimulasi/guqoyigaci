What is Patti D'Arbanville's Net Worth?

Patti D'Arbanville is an American actress and model who has a net worth of $2 million. Patti D'Arbanville was born in Manhattan, New York City, New York in May 1951. As an actress she starred on the television series Wiseguy in 1989. One of her best known roles came starring as Lt. Virginia Cooper on the TV series New York Undercover from 1994 to 1997.

From 1998 to 2000 Patti D'Arbanville starred as Selena Davis on the soap opera Guiding Light. From 2000 to 2005 she starred as Rose Boscorelli on the series Third Watch.

Katie Holmes rocks a blunt fringe in unrecognisable throwback photo

The talented actress posted a photo of her in character from the hit 2003 film Pieces of April

    Katie Holmes sure looks good with a fringe! The stunning actress took to her Instagram account on Monday to share a throwback photo from the film set of the 2003 comedy-drama film , in which she played the lead role of wayward April Burns. After being sent a polaroid of her in character, Katie couldn’t resist sharing it with fans. In the snap, the star rocks a short, blunt fringe – which framed her face beautifully - and can be seen pouting at the camera.

    Katie Holmes looked stunning in a throwback photo from the set of Pieces of April

    The photo, which was captioned: "Thank you @pott28 for sending me this photo from the set of #piecesofapril not quite sure what I was doing in this photo. Lol. But loved making this movie," went down a treat with fans, who adored her retro-inspired hair. "Those bangs," one exclaimed. "Those bangs!! Love it!" another added. Others were quick to praise Katie for her portrayal of April. "Oh my God. I love this movie so much. I named my daughter April many years after watching this cool movie. You were great."

    Katie often styles her glossy brunette hair in loose curls 

    Throughout the years, Katie has enjoyed experimenting with her hair for both film roles and during red carpet appearances, with styles ranging from an ultra-cute pixie cut to honey-tipped layering. Nowadays, the mother-of-one is often seen styling her shoulder-length hair in loose curls.

    Katie has been enjoying a summer holiday of late with her 11-year-old daughter Suri Cruise. Last week, the mother-daughter duo returned to Katie's hometown of Ohio to celebrate her sister's birthday. The notoriously private actress gave fans a glimpse into her home life by sharing a sweet family photo of her with her relatives, where she looked stylish in a ruffled blouse and white trousers, and kept her look casual by scraping her brunette hair up in a messy bun.

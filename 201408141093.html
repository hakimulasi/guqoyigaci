Scott Disick - Biography

Scott Disick is best known as Kourtney Kardashian's ex-partner

    "Whether it is Sir Disick, Lord Disick, or Count Disick, becoming royal is really gonna get the respect that I deserve. Like, I don't need to be walking around like some peasant" said Scott Disick on purchasing an English knighthood over the internet.

    It's just one of the many antics that makes his inclusion in Keeping Up with the Kardashians so much fun. Since getting together with Kourtney Kardashian in 2007, the New Yorker has been part of reality TV's first family.

    Career

    Scott was born on 26 May 1983 in the Big Apple as an only child. Before meeting Kourtney, he modeled for romantic novel covers and was renowned for having very expensive taste in clothes. Since then, the man about town has worked in several private businesses.

    “I have done a lot of private label manufacturing in the nutrition business and have raised money for startup companies. I tend to invest money into nightlife businesses for the friends of mine that are involved in nightclubs and what not,” he said about his business career.

    Relationship with the Kardashians

    On the TV series, Scott battled a series of issues related to anger management and partying. After their son Mason’s birth in 2009, he and Kourtney went through separated but ultimately reunited.With the result that his relationship with some of their relatives, especially Khloé was tough.

    "Before they would be like, if Scott knew Khloé was going to be there, he wouldn't come, if Khloé knew Scott was going to be there, she wouldn't come," Kourtney admitted. But later added that: “now they can be in the same room—they're definitely not calling each other like, 'I miss you so much, let's talk.' They don't speak, but they're civil. Nowadays, Scott and Khloé are known to be quite close.

    In 2010, Scott faced his issues, stating: "I had to take a look at where my life is going. Or where it was gonna go if I kept acting the way I was. I never ever wanted to admit that I may have a problem".

    Two years after they welcomed another member of the family, Penelope Disick. In 2014, Kourtney announced that she was expecting their third child, due in December 2014.

    The couple ultimately split up in 2015 but have shared custody and co-parent their children. He is still considered to be part of the family.

    Life after The Kardashians

    Two years after splitting from Kourtney, he was linked with Sofia Richie, then 19 years old. Between February and September 2021, Scott was in a relationship with Lisa Rinna's daughter Amelia Hamlin, then 19 years old.

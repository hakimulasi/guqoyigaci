Tom Berenger Second Wife: Who is Lisa Berenger?

Lisa Berenger is the second wife of American actor Tom Berenger. Tom Berenger wed Lisa Williams in 1986 and had three daughters named Chelsea, Chloe, and Shiloh. The couple divorced in 1997.

Tom Berenger is known for his remarkable performances in various movies and television shows. Berenger started his acting career in theatre and later shifted to film and television.

Berenger’s breakthrough role came in the 1986 war movie, “Platoon,” where he played the character of Sergeant Barnes. He received critical acclaim and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for his portrayal of the character.

He is also known for his roles in other popular movies like “Major League,” “The Big Chill,” “Inception,” “Born on the Fourth of July,” and “Gettysburg.”

Apart from movies, Berenger has also made appearances on various television shows. He played the role of Tim Sullivan in the TV series “Hatfields & McCoys,” for which he won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Miniseries or a Movie. He also appeared in “Cheers,” “Third Watch,” “Into the West,” and “Training Day.”

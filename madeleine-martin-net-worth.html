What is Madeleine Martin's Net Worth?

Madeleine Martin is an American actress who has a net worth of $4 million. Madeline Martin was born in Manhattan, New York City in April 1993. She is best known for playing the role of Rebecca "Becca" Moody on the TV series Californication. She appeared in a Broadway National Tour of The Sound of Music at the age of seven. Martin became the youngest presenter in history at the 2003 Tony Awards. Madeleine's first television appearance came in the series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit in 2004.

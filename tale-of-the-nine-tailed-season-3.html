Tale of the Nine Tailed Season 3:- Tale of the Nine Tailed is a popular South Korean fantasy drama series that follows the adventures of a nine-tailed fox named Lee Yeon, who has lived for centuries as a guardian of the human world. He falls in love with a fearless and curious television producer named Nam Ji Ah, who is investigating the mysterious cases involving urban legends. Together, they face various dangers and enemies, including Lee Yeon’s half-brother Lee Rang, who harbors a deep resentment towards him, and a powerful evil spirit named Imoogi, who wants to take over Lee Yeon’s body and Ji Ah’s soul.

The series has been praised for its captivating storyline, stunning visuals, and stellar performances by the cast, especially Lee Dong Wook, who plays Lee Yeon, and Jo Bo Ah, who plays Ji Ah. The series has also received high ratings and viewership, making it one of the most successful dramas of 2021 and 2022. The first season aired from October to December 2021, and the second season aired from February to April 2022, both on tvN and Disney+.

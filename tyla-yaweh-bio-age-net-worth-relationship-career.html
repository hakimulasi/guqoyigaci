On May 31, 1995, Tyla Yaweh was born in Orlando, Florida, in the United States. He was raised in an area where drug use and criminal activity were widespread. Tyla’s mother kicked him out of the family after he got involved in drug trafficking. Then, with his friend’s help, he moved from Florida to Los Angeles.

Tyla’s manager, Dre London, and Post Malone recognized him when he was living in Los Angeles. He was eventually signed to London Entertainment, Malone’s record label, because of his extraordinary musical talent.

How did Tyla Yaweh start his Professional Career?

Drugs and Pain, his debut single, was made available on November 22, 2017. The song’s official music video was made available on January 12, 2018. With a million views on YouTube, it was able to draw in a sizable audience of music lovers. He released a ton of new songs as a follow-up, such as “Wildlife,” “Goals,” and “Gemini.”

Millions of people listened to the three songs on streaming services like SoundCloud, YouTube, and Spotify.
